About Us

Home » About us

The Pragya Parmita Society is a result of the collaboration between reputed educationists, professionals, and visionaries from various apex institutions in the country. It is an initiative to provide wings to the creative aspirations of students. Education is neither a business nor a part-time activity for us. It is our passion, devotion, and a matter of existence. With the noble intention in our heart and dedication in our mind, we are here to provide innovative solutions for all academic and competitive examination needs.

Here Is How We Will Fulfill Our Promises

  • A team of highly experienced and competent experts, who will provide their specialties in there subject. And trust comes with their education from world-class institutes like IIT's and NIT's.
  • A specialized goal-oriented class to improve reasoning ability.
  • Focused and practical session for awareness of emerging technologies and current world scenarios.
  • A focused class for Imparting ethics, awareness of our rich cultural values, and overall personality development so that they become responsible citizens.
  • The expert made study material to ensure no stones unturned.
  • A continuous evaluation for timely feedback to orchestrate efficient improvement.
  • Supervised and rigorous practice session.
  • We are highly accessible with app, websites, and YouTube channels so that everyone gets their way of learning at any point of time.
  • Supervised study groups to help students to resolve all their problems beyond their class timing through different modes of communication.
  • Teacher-student ratio is as such that it provides personalized learning with an easy one to one interaction.
