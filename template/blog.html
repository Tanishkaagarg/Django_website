About pragyaparamita

The Pragya Parmita Society is a result of the collaboration between reputed educationists, professionals, and visionaries from various apex institutions in the country. It is an initiative to provide wings to the creative aspirations of students. Education is neither a business nor a part-time activity for us.

It is our passion, devotion, and a matter of existence. With the noble intention in our heart and dedication in our mind, we are here to provide innovative solutions for all academic and competitive examination needs.
